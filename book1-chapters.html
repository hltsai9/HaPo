Seventeen scenes — one for each chapter of Philosopher's Stone. Hover for a reminder of what happens.

Chapter 1: The Boy Who Lived

Dumbledore, McGonagall, and Hagrid leave a scarred baby Harry on the Dursleys' doorstep under a starlit Privet Drive.

Chapter 2: The Vanishing Glass

At the zoo, Harry accidentally vanishes the glass of the snake's enclosure — and the boa constrictor slips out thanking him.

Chapter 3: The Letters from No One

Hogwarts letters find Harry everywhere — up the chimney, through the cracks, by the dozens no matter how Vernon tries to stop them.

Chapter 4: The Keeper of the Keys

Hagrid smashes down the door of the hut on the rock, hands Harry a birthday cake, and announces: "Yer a wizard, Harry."

Chapter 5: Diagon Alley

Wands, cauldrons, owls, robes, and one very tall goblin-run bank — Hagrid shows Harry the whole shimmering street behind the brick wall.

Chapter 6: The Journey from Platform Nine and Three-Quarters

The Hogwarts Express steams out of King's Cross. Harry meets Ron, trades sweets, and a bushy-haired girl demands to know if anyone has seen a toad.

Chapter 7: The Sorting Hat

A patched, pointed old hat opens its brim, sings a song, and sorts each first-year into a house. For Harry it whispers "Not Slytherin, eh?"

Chapter 8: The Potions Master

In a chilly dungeon, Snape grills Harry about asphodel, bezoars, and monkshood — then docks Gryffindor its first point of the year.

Chapter 9: The Midnight Duel

Harry dives fifty feet to catch Neville's Remembrall and is made Gryffindor's Seeker — the youngest in a century.

Chapter 10: Hallowe'en

"Troll — in the dungeons!" Harry and Ron knock it out with its own club and earn Hermione's loyalty forever.

Chapter 11: Quidditch

Harry's first match. He swallows the Snitch to win — and notices Snape muttering a curse on his broomstick.

Chapter 12: The Mirror of Erised

Harry sees his smiling parents and long-lost family for the first time — and Dumbledore warns that "men have wasted away before it."

Chapter 13: Nicolas Flamel

Hermione finally finds Flamel — the only known maker of the Philosopher's Stone, an elixir of life, and the thing Fluffy is guarding.

Chapter 14: Norbert the Norwegian Ridgeback

Hagrid hatches a dragon in his wooden hut. Norbert is small, spiky, and already setting fires — until Charlie's friends smuggle him to Romania.

Chapter 15: The Forbidden Forest

On detention, Harry finds a unicorn bleeding silver — and a hooded figure drinking. Firenze the centaur rescues him just in time.

Chapter 16: Through the Trapdoor

Harry, Ron, and Hermione soothe Fluffy with a harp's music and drop through the trapdoor to face Devil's Snare, flying keys, and a chess board.

Chapter 17: The Man with Two Faces

Not Snape — Quirrell. He unwraps his turban, revealing Voldemort's face on the back of his head. The Stone protects itself; Harry survives.
