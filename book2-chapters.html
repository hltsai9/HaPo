Eighteen scenes — one per chapter of Chamber of Secrets.

Chapter 1: The Worst Birthday

It's Harry's twelfth birthday and nobody has remembered. The Dursleys lock him in the cupboard and pretend he doesn't exist.

Chapter 2: Dobby's Warning

A small creature with enormous eyes and a filthy pillowcase appears in Harry's room: "Harry Potter must not go back to Hogwarts!"

Chapter 3: The Burrow

Ron, Fred, and George roar up in a flying turquoise Ford Anglia and tear the bars off Harry's window. Off to the Weasleys' home.

Chapter 4: At Flourish and Blotts

Gilderoy Lockhart, in a sickening turquoise cloak, signs autographs — while Lucius Malfoy slips a cursed diary into Ginny's cauldron.

Chapter 5: The Whomping Willow

The flying Ford Anglia lands in Hogwarts grounds — right in the branches of a very, very violent tree.

Chapter 6: Gilderoy Lockhart

The new DADA professor signs Harry up as his "co-star," lets loose a cage of Cornish pixies, and proves magnificently useless.

Chapter 7: Mudbloods and Murmurs

Draco calls Hermione a Mudblood. Ron's "Eat Slugs!" curse backfires through his broken wand — and he spends the day vomiting them up.

Chapter 8: The Deathday Party

Sir Nicholas invites the trio to his 500th Deathday Party — a freezing dungeon of moldy food, wailing ghosts, and his dangling head.

Chapter 9: The Writing on the Wall

Mrs. Norris hangs stiff as a board from a torch bracket. Above her, a dripping warning: the Chamber of Secrets has been opened.

Chapter 10: The Rogue Bludger

A Bludger chases Harry all over the pitch, breaks his arm, and Lockhart's mending charm removes every bone in it.

Chapter 11: The Dueling Club

Lockhart and Snape "demonstrate." Malfoy conjures a snake. Harry hisses to it in Parseltongue — and the school decides he's the Heir.

Chapter 12: The Polyjuice Potion

A month of brewing in Moaning Myrtle's bathroom. Harry becomes Goyle, Ron becomes Crabbe — and Hermione accidentally becomes a cat.

Chapter 13: The Very Secret Diary

Harry writes in Riddle's blank diary. The ink disappears into the page — and flowing script answers back: "Hello, Harry Potter."

Chapter 14: Cornelius Fudge

The Minister of Magic arrives at Hagrid's cabin with an order: Hagrid must be taken to Azkaban. And Lucius Malfoy suspends Dumbledore.

Chapter 15: Aragog

Following spiders into the Forbidden Forest, Harry and Ron meet Hagrid's old pet — a car-sized acromantula, blind, ancient, and quite hungry.

Chapter 16: The Chamber of Secrets

Moaning Myrtle's bathroom. Harry hisses "Open" at the tap engraved with a tiny snake — and the sinks slide apart to reveal a pit.

Chapter 17: The Heir of Slytherin

Harry slays the basilisk with Gryffindor's sword, then drives a fang through Tom Riddle's diary — and saves Ginny's life.

Chapter 18: Dobby's Reward

"Dobby is free!" Harry slips his smelly sock into the diary. Lucius throws it, Dobby catches it — and a house-elf is liberated.
