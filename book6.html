Harry Potter & the Half-Blood Prince

A Character Guide to Book 6

Sixth year. Dumbledore takes Harry on a private investigation of Voldemort's past; Snape teaches Defense Against the Dark Arts at last; Draco has a secret mission; and a battered Potions textbook falls into Harry's hands, signed "the Half-Blood Prince."

Gryffindor

Captain, Keeper, and a Potions Prince

Harry Potter

Quidditch captain, top of Potions thanks to a stranger's book, drinker of Felix Felicis. Becomes Dumbledore's confidant and his last apprentice.

Ron Weasley

Eats love-potion-laced chocolates meant for Harry, then dates Lavender Brown ("Won-Won"). Survives a poisoned mead Christmas drink — and finally sees Hermione cry.

Hermione Granger

Sets her enchanted canaries on Ron after the Lavender debacle. Translates Tales of Beedle the Bard, decodes the Half-Blood Prince — and finally cries for him.

Ginny Weasley

Top Chaser, Bat-Bogey extraordinaire. Slowly steals Harry's heart, then steals a kiss in the common room after a Quidditch win.

Slytherin

A boy with a mission, a master with a vow

Draco Malfoy

Branded with the Dark Mark in his father's place. Spends the year haunting the Room of Requirement, mending the Vanishing Cabinet — and crying in Myrtle's bathroom.

Professor Snape

Finally gets the Defense post. Made an Unbreakable Vow to Narcissa — to finish the job if Draco fails. Casts the curse that ends Dumbledore.

The Old Sluggish Master

Horace Slughorn

Returning Potions Master. Collects famous students like party favours; runs the Slug Club from his armchair. Holds the memory Voldemort doesn't want anyone to see.

Beyond the Houses

Albus Dumbledore

His withered, blackened hand from a Horcrux's curse hides under the table all year. Takes Harry to a cave by the sea — and onto the Astronomy Tower.

Fleur Delacour

Engaged to Bill Weasley and dismissed by his mother as "Phlegm." Sticks by Bill after a werewolf attack ravages his face — and earns Molly's apology.

The Inferi

Drowned corpses bewitched by Voldemort to guard the locket Horcrux at the bottom of the cave's lake. They drag Harry under — and Dumbledore burns them away.
