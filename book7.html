Harry Potter & the Deathly Hallows

A Character Guide to Book 7

Seventeen years old. No more Hogwarts, no more Dumbledore. The hunt for Horcruxes, the legend of the Deathly Hallows, and the Battle of Hogwarts. Plus, nineteen years later.

Gryffindor

The Trio, the army, and the boy who lived

Harry Potter

The Chosen One. Carries the locket Horcrux through forests and fells. Walks willingly to his own death — and chooses to come back.

Ron Weasley

Walks out on Harry, then walks back in to save him from a frozen pool. Pulls the silver doe from a memory of his best friend's love.

Hermione Granger

Erases her parents' memories of her, packs an entire library into a beaded bag, and figures out the Deathly Hallows. The reason any of them survive.

Neville Longbottom

Leads Dumbledore's Army through Snape's reign of terror. Pulls the Sword of Gryffindor from the Sorting Hat — and beheads Nagini.

Ginny Weasley

Held back from the road by Mrs. Weasley but never away from the fight. Reforms the DA. Faints, dances, kisses Harry in his old bedroom — then waits.

Slytherin

Always

Severus Snape

Headmaster of Hogwarts. The bravest man Harry ever knew. His Patronus is a silver doe — Lily's. He has loved her always.

The Order, the Allies, the Dead

Dobby

Apparates Harry and friends out of Malfoy Manor — and dies in Harry's arms on the beach by Shell Cottage. "Such a beautiful place to be with friends."

The Three Brothers

Cloak, Stone, Wand. Hidden in plain sight as a children's tale. The unbeatable Elder Wand, the Resurrection Stone, the Cloak of Invisibility — the Deathly Hallows.

Lord Voldemort

Storms Hogwarts at last with the Elder Wand. But the wand answers to its true master — and Avada Kedavra rebounds, for good.

Bill & Fleur Weasley

Married at the Burrow. Their wedding ends in chaos when Kingsley's Patronus brings the news: the Ministry has fallen.

Xenophilius Lovegood

Luna's father. Wears the Deathly Hallows symbol around his neck and tells the trio the legend — then betrays them, desperate for his daughter's return.

Griphook

The Gringotts goblin who agrees to help break into the Lestrange vault — for the Sword of Gryffindor — and betrays them at the moment of truth.

The Ukrainian Ironbelly

The blind, half-mad dragon chained beneath Gringotts. Harry, Ron, and Hermione climb onto its back and ride it free into the open sky.

Nineteen Years Later

The Next Generation

King's Cross, nineteen years on. Albus Severus, Rose Weasley, James Potter — boarding the Hogwarts Express. All was well.
