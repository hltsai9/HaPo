Harry Potter & the Order of the Phoenix

A Character Guide to Book 5

Fifth year. Voldemort is back, but the Ministry refuses to admit it. A pink-clad inquisitor descends on Hogwarts, an old Order rises again, and the Department of Mysteries holds a prophecy with Harry's name on it.

Gryffindor

Dumbledore's Army

Harry Potter

Furious, frustrated, and fifteen. Founds Dumbledore's Army, suffers Umbridge's blood-quill detentions, and loses Sirius in the Department of Mysteries.

Ron Weasley

Made prefect (over Harry's head). Fumbles his way into the Gryffindor Keeper position; "Weasley is our King" is sung two ways before the year is out.

Hermione Granger

Brews stoppered bluebell flames, organises the DA, jinxes the membership parchment to mark traitors, and decks the centaurs into rescuing them.

Neville Longbottom

Practises every DA lesson harder than anyone. Tells Harry the truth: his parents were tortured to madness by Bellatrix. Stands beside him in the Department of Mysteries.

Ginny Weasley

Bursts onto the page with her famous Bat-Bogey Hex. Joins the DA, joins the Department of Mysteries fight, no longer the silent little sister.

Slytherin

Death Eaters reborn

Bellatrix Lestrange

Just escaped from Azkaban, mad with devotion to her master. Tortured the Longbottoms into madness — and casts the curse that sends Sirius through the Veil.

Draco Malfoy

Top of Umbridge's new Inquisitorial Squad. Spends the year docking points and toadying — until his father Lucius is sent to Azkaban.

Ravenclaw

"Loony" Luna and the Wrackspurts

Luna Lovegood

Wears radish earrings and a butterbeer-cork necklace, reads The Quibbler upside down, believes in Crumple-Horned Snorkacks. The first to see the thestrals — and the truest friend.

Cho Chang

Grieving Cedric. Cries through her first kiss with Harry, betrays the DA through her friend Marietta's mistake, and slowly drifts apart from him.

The Order of the Phoenix

Sirius Black

Trapped in the house he grew up hating. Treats Harry like a son and like a friend in equal measure — and falls through the Veil with a look of surprise.

Nymphadora Tonks

Auror, Metamorphmagus, hopelessly clumsy. Bubblegum-pink hair when she's happy. Don't ever, ever call her Nymphadora.

Kingsley Shacklebolt

Tall, deep-voiced Auror in plum robes and a single gold hoop. The Order's man inside the Auror office hunting for Sirius — and falsifying every report.

Mundungus Fletcher

Petty crook in the Order's payroll. Smells of pipe smoke and old fish. Falls asleep on guard duty when Harry needs him most.

Beyond the Houses

Dolores Umbridge

Senior Undersecretary, High Inquisitor, then Headmistress. Toad-faced, ribbon-bowed, sweet as poison. Her detentions carve "I must not tell lies" into Harry's hand.

Firenze

The centaur who saved Harry in the Forbidden Forest. Banished by his herd for accepting Dumbledore's job — and teaches Divination from a starlit indoor forest.

Kreacher

The Black family's ancient, malevolent house-elf. Mutters insults under his breath, hides his mistress's portrait, and ultimately betrays Sirius to Bellatrix.

Grawp

Hagrid's sixteen-foot-tall little half-brother. Tied to the Forbidden Forest, fond of pulling up trees, and unexpectedly fond of "Hermy."
