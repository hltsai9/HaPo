Twenty-two scenes — one per chapter of Prisoner of Azkaban.

Chapter 1: Owl Post

On Harry's 13th birthday, three owls bring gifts from Ron, Hermione, and Hagrid — the Sneakoscope, the Broomstick Servicing Kit, and the Monster Book.

Chapter 2: Aunt Marge's Big Mistake

Harry's temper inflates Aunt Marge like a giant purple balloon. She drifts up, up, and out of the house — and Harry grabs his trunk and flees.

Chapter 3: The Knight Bus

The violently purple triple-decker appears with a BANG. Stan Shunpike waves Harry aboard — "Welcome to the Knight Bus."

Chapter 4: The Leaky Cauldron

Fudge himself sees Harry settled in at the Leaky Cauldron. For nearly two weeks Harry has London to himself — until the new term begins.

Chapter 5: The Dementor

On the Hogwarts Express, the lamps die, windows frost, and a hooded horror glides in. Harry hears his mother scream before he collapses.

Chapter 6: Talons and Tea Leaves

Trelawney sees the Grim in Harry's teacup. Outside, Hagrid introduces Buckbeak — who bites Draco for being rude.

Chapter 7: The Boggart in the Wardrobe

Lupin's first lesson. Neville faces a Boggart-Snape — now in grandmother's green dress, vulture hat, and bright red handbag.

Chapter 8: Flight of the Fat Lady

Return from the Halloween feast: the portrait guarding Gryffindor Tower is slashed to ribbons. The Fat Lady has fled — Sirius Black has been inside the castle.

Chapter 9: Grim Defeat

In a thunderstorm, Dementors swarm the pitch. Harry falls fifty feet; Dumbledore slows him just in time — but his Nimbus is lost to the Whomping Willow.

Chapter 10: The Marauder's Map

Fred and George gift Harry the Marauder's Map — a parchment that shows every soul in Hogwarts, by name, in real time.

Chapter 11: The Firebolt

A state-of-the-art broomstick arrives for Harry on Christmas morning — unsigned. McGonagall promptly confiscates it for inspection.

Chapter 12: The Patronus

"Expecto Patronum!" Lupin teaches Harry the charm. A great silver stag bursts from his wand — his father James's Animagus form, Prongs.

Chapter 13: Gryffindor versus Ravenclaw

On his new Firebolt, Harry catches the Snitch and — when "Dementors" appear on the pitch — casts his first full Patronus at them (Malfoy and friends in robes).

Chapter 14: Snape's Grudge

Snape corners Harry clutching a blank parchment — the Map insults him in its makers' voices. Lupin arrives, quietly confiscates the Map, and saves Harry.

Chapter 15: The Quidditch Final

Gryffindor versus Slytherin. Harry seizes the Snitch right under Draco's nose — and Gryffindor wins the Cup for the first time in seven years.

Chapter 16: Professor Trelawney's Prediction

Mid-exam, Trelawney goes into a trance and makes her second-ever real prophecy: the Dark Lord's servant will return tonight.

Chapter 17: Cat, Rat, and Dog

A ginger cat, a terrified rat, and an enormous black dog meet at the Whomping Willow. The dog drags Ron down a tunnel by his leg.

Chapter 18: Moony, Wormtail, Padfoot & Prongs

In the Shrieking Shack, Lupin explains the Marauders: Remus the werewolf, Peter the rat, Sirius the dog, and James the stag — four Animagi who changed everything.

Chapter 19: The Servant of Lord Voldemort

The rat is forced back into a man: Peter Pettigrew, missing a finger, betrayer of Lily and James. He was Voldemort's servant all along.

Chapter 20: The Dementor's Kiss

By the black lake, a hundred Dementors close in on Harry and Sirius. Then, from across the water, a blinding silver stag charges — and drives them all away.

Chapter 21: Hermione's Secret

Hermione's Time-Turner. Three turns back, and she and Harry fly Buckbeak up to a high window, rescuing Sirius from the Dementor's Kiss.

Chapter 22: Owl Post Again

On the train home, a tiny bullet-sized owl (Pigwidgeon) delivers a letter from Sirius — signing the Firebolt permission slip and giving Ron a new pet.
