Book 6: Chapter Illustrations

Harry Potter & the Half-Blood Prince — a scene from each chapter

Thirty scenes — one per chapter of Half-Blood Prince.

Chapter 1: The Other Minister

Cornelius Fudge breaks the news to the Muggle Prime Minister: the wizarding world is at war again — and his successor is on the way.

Chapter 2: Spinner's End

Bellatrix watches as Snape and Narcissa make the Unbreakable Vow: he will protect Draco — and finish the task if Draco fails.

Chapter 3: Will and Won't

Dumbledore arrives at Privet Drive, breaks the news of Sirius's will, and brings Kreacher to Harry.

Chapter 4: Horace Slughorn

Slughorn has Transfigured himself into an armchair to escape Death Eater notice. Dumbledore prods the cushion with his wand.

Chapter 5: An Excess of Phlegm

Bill brings Fleur to the Burrow for the summer. Mrs. Weasley and Ginny brace for "Phlegm" at every meal.

Chapter 6: Draco's Detour

Harry, hidden in the upstairs window, sees Draco slip into Borgin and Burkes — to mend a Vanishing Cabinet.

Chapter 7: The Slug Club

Slughorn's first compartment party on the Hogwarts Express — crystallised pineapple, name-dropping, and a notable invite list.

Chapter 8: Snape Victorious

Dumbledore announces it at the start-of-term feast: Snape takes Defense Against the Dark Arts, Slughorn takes Potions.

Chapter 9: The Half-Blood Prince

The battered Potions textbook from the cupboard. Margins crammed with corrections, hexes — and a name in the cover.

Chapter 10: The House of Gaunt

Bob Ogden's memory: the rotten Gaunt shack with a dead snake nailed to the door — the last Gaunts of Slytherin.

Chapter 11: Hermione's Helping Hand

Hermione jinxes Cormac McLaggen at the Keeper tryouts so Ron wins the spot. She just can't bring herself to admit it.

Chapter 12: Silver and Opals

On a Hogsmeade weekend, an Imperiused Katie Bell touches a cursed opal necklace and rises screaming into the air.

Chapter 13: The Secret Riddle

Memory after memory: a strange child in an orphanage who can hurt others without touching them. Tom Riddle's first appearance.

Chapter 14: Felix Felicis

Slughorn awards Harry his prize — twelve hours of liquid luck in a tiny gold vial. Harry decides to save it.

Chapter 15: The Unbreakable Vow

The Vow itself: three flaming threads weaving from Snape's wand around the joined hands, sealing his oath in his very soul.

Chapter 16: A Very Frosty Christmas

Christmas at the Burrow: Percy still absent, Scrimgeour visits hoping to recruit Harry, Mrs. Weasley clings tighter than ever.

Chapter 17: A Sluggish Memory

The memory Dumbledore needs from Slughorn — but the old professor has tampered with it. The truth is hidden in the smudge.

Chapter 18: Birthday Surprises

Ron's seventeenth. He drinks Slughorn's poisoned mead. Harry remembers a goat's stomach stone — and shoves a bezoar down his throat.

Chapter 19: Elf Tails

Harry sets Dobby and Kreacher to tail Draco. Dobby is delighted; Kreacher mutters insults the entire time.

Chapter 20: Lord Voldemort's Request

A grown Tom Riddle returns to Hogwarts to ask for the Defense Against the Dark Arts post. Dumbledore refuses — and curses the job.

Chapter 21: The Unknowable Room

Harry knows Draco is in the Room of Requirement, but cannot guess what version Draco has asked it to become.

Chapter 22: After the Burial

Aragog dies. Hagrid buries him. Slughorn comes for the wake — and his memory at last loosens with a glass of Felix Felicis.

Chapter 23: Horcruxes

The truth at last. Slughorn's memory — Voldemort split his soul into seven pieces, hidden in objects across the world.

Chapter 24: Sectumsempra

The Half-Blood Prince's "For Enemies" curse opens Draco like a sword. Harry watches in horror — Snape arrives just in time to save him.

Chapter 25: The Seer Overheard

Trelawney recounts the night she made the prophecy — and Harry realises Snape was the eavesdropper who carried it to Voldemort.

Chapter 26: The Cave

Across a black lake to an island of green-glowing potion. Dumbledore drinks; Inferi rise; Harry conjures fire to drive them off.

Chapter 27: The Lightning-Struck Tower

The Dark Mark blazes above Hogwarts. Atop the Astronomy Tower, Draco corners Dumbledore with a wand he cannot bring himself to use.

Chapter 28: Flight of the Prince

Snape, fleeing across the Hogwarts grounds, blocks every curse Harry throws — and finally screams the truth: he is the Half-Blood Prince.

Chapter 29: The Phoenix Lament

Fawkes sings a lament in the dawn over Hogwarts. The school mourns; the survivors gather in the hospital wing.

Chapter 30: The White Tomb

By the lake, Dumbledore is laid to rest. The centaurs salute with arrows. Harry, Ron, and Hermione decide: they're going Horcrux hunting.
